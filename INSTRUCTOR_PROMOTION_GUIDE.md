## How to Promote a Member

### Option 1: Individual Promotion (Recommended)

1. **Login** to admin portal
2. **Navigate** to `/admin/promotions`
3. **Find member** using filters or search
4. **Click "Promote"** on member row
5. **Choose promotion type**:
   - **Stripe Promotion**: Add 1 stripe (if < 4 stripes)
   - **Belt Promotion**: Advance to next belt
6. **Add notes** (technique highlights, attitude, etc.)
7. **Click "Promote"**
8. **Done!** Member gets notification

### Option 2: Batch Promotion (Belt Ceremonies)

1. **Navigate** to `/admin/promotions`
2. **Filter** by program/belt as needed
3. **Check "Show Eligible Only"**
4. **Select members** to promote (checkboxes)
5. **Click "Promote Selected"**
6. **Choose type**: Stripe or Belt
7. **Add ceremony notes** (optional)
8. **Submit batch promotion**

## Stripe Promotions

Stripes recognize progress within a belt rank.

**When to award stripes:**
- Member shows improvement in specific areas
- Consistent attendance and effort
- Learning new techniques
- Helping training partners
- Attitude and dedication

**Stripe Guidelines:**
- 1st Stripe: Basic understanding
- 2nd Stripe: Growing competence
- 3rd Stripe: Consistent performance
- 4th Stripe: Ready for next belt soon

**Note**: 4 stripes is NOT required before belt promotion, but is common.

## Special Situations

### Transitioning Kids to Adults (Age 16)

When kid turns 16:
1. Assess current skill level
2. Typically start at white or blue belt
3. Kids green belt â‰ˆ Adult white/blue
4. Focus on fundamentals

### Member Returning After Break

If member returns after 6+ months away:
1. Review current belt requirements
2. May need time to regain skill
3. Don't demote, but delay next promotion
4. Reset promotion timer if needed

### Competition Success

Winning tournaments can accelerate promotions:
- Gold medal at belt level: +25% credit
- Multiple golds: Strong indicator
- But competition NOT required

### Injury/Health Issues

For members with injuries:
- Focus on technique knowledge
- Participation when able counts
- May promote based on understanding
- Show compassion and flexibility

## Promotion Ceremony Tips

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I skip a belt?**
A: No. System prevents skipping belts per IBJJF rules.

**Q: Can I promote someone with less than minimum time?**
A: Exceptional cases only. Contact head instructor.

**Q: Member has 4 stripes but not ready for next belt?**
A: Keep them at 4 stripes. No need to add more.

**Q: Can I remove stripes?**
A: No. Stripes are earned and kept.

**Q: What if I promote wrong member by accident?**
A: Contact admin immediately to correct in database.

**Q: Can parents promote their own kids?**
A: Yes, but get second opinion from another instructor.
